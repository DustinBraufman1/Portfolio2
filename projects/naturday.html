Naturday Rebrand

Project Overview

In class rebrand of Anheuser-Busch's lemonade beer "Naturday". Intended on repositioning the product to appeal to hard seltzer fans while keeping the fun nature of the original design intact. Project was awarded an honorable mention in AIGA Philadelphia's LOCALLY SOURCED Issue 04.​​​​​​​
